# Project Brief: Garrit & Wulf Auto Parts Website

## Project Overview
A modern, professional website for Garrit & Wulf, an auto parts supplier based in Dubai, UAE. The project focuses on showcasing their premium auto parts across European, American, and Truck categories with a sophisticated dark theme and maroon brand color.

## Core Objectives
1. **Brand Presence**: Establish a premium online presence for Garrit & Wulf
2. **Product Showcase**: Display auto parts categories with engaging visuals
3. **Customer Contact**: Provide easy access to contact information and inquiry forms
4. **Modern Design**: Implement contemporary dark theme with maroon (#6e0000) accent color
5. **User Experience**: Ensure responsive, fast, and intuitive navigation

## Target Audience
- Auto repair shops and mechanics in Dubai/UAE
- Vehicle fleet managers
- Auto parts wholesalers and retailers
- Individual vehicle owners seeking quality parts

## Key Requirements
- Next.js 14 with App Router
- TypeScript for type safety
- Tailwind CSS for styling
- Responsive design (mobile, tablet, desktop)
- SEO-optimized pages
- Fast loading times with optimized images
- Professional contact form with validation
- Google Maps integration for location

## Success Criteria
- Modern, professional appearance matching industry standards
- Consistent brand color (#6e0000) throughout
- Fast page load times (<3 seconds)
- Mobile-responsive on all devices
- Clear call-to-action buttons
- Easy navigation and information access
- Professional presentation of company values and products
